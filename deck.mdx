# Negatives of the job

<Steps>

- Telling devs that things are not working
- Dealing with crazy timelines
- Avoiding overwork
- Remaining confident and motivated
- Staying on top of new trends in the QA Space

</Steps>

Tips to get into the QA Space

<Steps>

- Market yourself
- Hone necessary skills - Testers mindset, attention to detail, Being analytical
- Continously learn
- Do not be afraid to go outside of your comfort zone
- Improve your communication skills

</Steps>
